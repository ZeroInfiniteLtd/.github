## 🚀 **About Zero Infinite Ltd**

Zero Infinite Ltd is a UK‑based technology company focused on building **automated, resilient, and self‑maintaining digital systems**.  
Our mission is simple: remove the burden of maintenance so organisations can focus on innovation, scale, and long‑term growth.

We combine **enterprise engineering**, **modern cloud architecture**, and **cutting‑edge research** — including hands‑on experience developing solutions for **quantum computers**, showcased in our founder’s personal work:  
🔗 **AI and Quantum Projects by Shiraz Adam** — [https://github.com/shirazadam/](https://github.com/shirazadam/)

## 🧭 **What We Offer (Business‑Focused Overview)**

We help organisations modernise, automate, and secure their technology landscape through:

### **Cloud Architecture & Automation**
- Azure‑native, scalable, secure environments  
- Zero‑touch deployment pipelines  
- Cost‑optimised, resilient infrastructure  

### **DevOps & CI/CD Engineering**
- Azure DevOps & GitHub Actions pipelines  
- Automated testing, deployment, and monitoring  
- Containerisation and orchestration (Docker, Kubernetes)

### **System Integration**
- API‑driven architectures  
- Microservices and event‑driven systems  
- Legacy modernisation and interoperability  

### **Security & Compliance**
- Identity and access management  
- Audit, governance, and defence‑grade security patterns  
- Secure‑by‑design architecture  

### **AI & Data Solutions**
- Azure AI, cognitive services, and automation  
- Data pipelines, analytics, and intelligent workflows  
- Predictive and adaptive business systems  

Our solutions are engineered to deliver **zero maintenance overhead** and **infinite scalability**.
